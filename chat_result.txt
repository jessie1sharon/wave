Operating Instructions:
Frequencies:
Alpha Frequency - When one of the following words appears, release, calm, relax, breathe, forgive, or words from the same root
Beta Frequency - When one of the following words appears, think, listen, learn, stand up or words from the same root
Theta Frequency - When one of the following words appears, imagine, invest, quiet, stop, or words from the same root

Lights:
When referring to lights, appropriate lights will be activated in the capsule, and when calming is desired, the lights in the capsule will dim
When a decrease in light is described, we will lower the light in the capsule from top to bottom, and if an increase in light is described, we will raise the lights slowly and gently from bottom to top

Scents:
Lavender to create a sense of calmness
Geranium or ginger or citrus fruits to uplift the mood and provide a sense of optimism
Cinnamon for a sense of control and proper body activity
Rose scent for relaxation and a good mood state
Patchouli for better meditation state

Temperature:
When the text describes a decrease of any kind, we will slowly lower the temperature to make it noticeable
And accordingly for increasing the temperature
